Why SAVOUR?

In today's fast-paced world, choosing where and what to eat has become an increasingly frustrating experience. Many individuals find themselves repeatedly visiting the same restaurants and cafes, not out of preference, but due to the overwhelming number of choices and lack of personalized guidance. This decision fatigue often leads to missed opportunities to explore new culinary experiences, hidden gems, or trending hotspots.

SAVOUR addresses this gap with a smarter, more intuitive solution that understands your tastes, leverages your past behavior, and considers real-time location data to deliver meaningful, personalized dining recommendations.

Features

Map-based Restaurant Locator

Explore an interactive map powered by OpenStreetMap. See all nearby food & beverage spots within a configurable radius, filter in real-time, and navigate seamlessly.

Restaurant Deep Research Agentic AI

Our agentic AI system leverages deep web search and reflection to extract and analyze restaurant info—food recommendations, promotions, dietary options—so you can make informed choices.

Conversational Search with LLM

Use natural language prompts to search for restaurants by dietary needs, budget, cuisine, and more. Our LLM-powered semantic search makes discovery intuitive and personal.

User Access Management

Secure registration and login with Supabase. A public landing page guides new users through onboarding and app access.

Homepage Recommender

Get tailored dining suggestions on your homepage, based on your previous activity and preferences. Quick, relevant, and always fresh.

Profile & Personalisation Control

Customize your experience with a dedicated profile panel. Set dietary needs, cuisine preferences, and memory settings to supercharge your recommendations.
